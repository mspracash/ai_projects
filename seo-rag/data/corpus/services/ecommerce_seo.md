## title: E-commerce SEO Overview

E-commerce SEO focuses on optimizing online stores to improve visibility, traffic, and conversions for product and category pages.

Unlike standard SEO, e-commerce websites present unique challenges due to large inventories, dynamic content, and complex navigation systems.

## title: Faceted Navigation Optimization for E-commerce SEO
description: Controls indexing and crawl behavior of filtered pages to improve crawl efficiency and focus on high-value pages.
keywords: faceted navigation SEO, filtered pages, crawl efficiency, noindex, canonicalization, parameter URLs, ecommerce filters SEO

E-commerce SEO faceted navigation optimization focuses on controlling how filtered pages are indexed.

Faceted navigation can create thousands of URL variations based on filters such as size, color, or price.

This includes:

- applying noindex tags where appropriate  
- using canonicalization  
- controlling parameter-based URLs  

This ensures that search engines focus on high-value pages while maintaining usability.
