# Case Study: Home Services GBP Incomplete

## Client Snapshot
A regional plumbing company relied heavily on local search leads.
Despite strong service quality, the company appeared inconsistently in local pack results.

## Initial Symptoms
Search queries for plumbing services showed competitors dominating map results.
The client’s profile appeared only occasionally in local packs.

## Observed Evidence
Google Business Profile lacked service descriptions and images.
Only a small number of reviews existed compared with competitors.
The business categories were also overly generic.

## Technical Evidence
GBP insights showed low engagement rates.
Search queries containing service keywords rarely triggered the listing.
Competitor profiles included more detailed descriptions and photos.

## Hypothesis
Weak prominence and relevance signals prevented consistent local pack visibility.

## Recommended Services
GBP optimization
Service description updates
Photo additions
Review acquisition campaign

## Execution
Profile descriptions were rewritten with relevant service keywords.
Photos of completed projects were uploaded.
Customers were encouraged to submit reviews after completed jobs.

## Outcome
Within eight weeks the profile began appearing more consistently in local results.
Calls from GBP increased by roughly 35 percent.

## Lessons Learned
Local service companies often overlook profile completeness.
Regular updates improve both ranking signals and user engagement.
