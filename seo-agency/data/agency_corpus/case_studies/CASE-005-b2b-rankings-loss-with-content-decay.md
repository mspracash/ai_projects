# Case Study: B2B Rankings Loss With Content Decay

## Client Snapshot
A B2B software provider relied heavily on blog content for organic lead generation.
Traffic had been stable for several years before gradually declining.

## Initial Symptoms
Keyword rankings slowly dropped across many informational pages.
Organic traffic declined about 30 percent over one year.

## Observed Evidence
Many articles had not been updated in several years.
Competitors published more recent and comprehensive resources.

## Technical Evidence
Content analysis showed outdated statistics and weak internal linking.
Search Console revealed declining impressions for long‑tail keywords.

## Hypothesis
Content decay reduced relevance for competitive informational queries.

## Recommended Services
Keyword research refresh
Content updates
On‑page optimization improvements

## Execution
Analysts updated outdated statistics and examples.
New internal links were added between related articles.
Page titles and headings were optimized with updated keyword research.

## Outcome
Several updated articles returned to page one rankings.
Organic traffic increased within three months.

## Lessons Learned
Content requires periodic updates to remain competitive.
Informational search results reward freshness and depth.
