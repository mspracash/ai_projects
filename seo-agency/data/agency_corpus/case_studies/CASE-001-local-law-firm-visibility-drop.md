# Case Study: Local Law Firm Visibility Drop

## Client Snapshot
A regional law firm with offices in three cities contacted the agency after noticing a steady drop in calls from Google search results.
Historically the firm generated most new clients through local pack visibility.
For several years their listings appeared consistently within the top three positions.
During the previous six months that visibility began declining across multiple locations.
The marketing coordinator suspected competitors were gaining an advantage.

## Initial Symptoms
Keyword tracking tools showed a decline in local pack rankings.
Queries such as "family lawyer near me" and "estate planning attorney" dropped from top positions to page two.
Google Business Profile insights also showed fewer direction requests and phone calls.
Organic traffic to location pages declined by roughly 28 percent.

## Observed Evidence
Analysts reviewed the firm's Google Business Profile listings.
Several listings lacked updated categories and service descriptions.
The firm had very few recent customer reviews.
Competitors in the same market had accumulated hundreds of reviews and posted regular updates.
Location landing pages also contained minimal geographic content.

## Technical Evidence
Google Search Console revealed declining impressions for location‑based keywords.
Local landing pages had weak internal linking from the main site navigation.
Structured data for local business entities was missing from the site.
Page titles also lacked city‑specific keyword variations.
These factors weakened local relevance signals.

## Hypothesis
The team concluded that weakened local prominence signals were responsible.
Competitors had stronger review activity and more complete listings.
The firm's outdated profiles likely reduced their eligibility for local pack positions.

## Recommended Services
Local SEO foundation work
Google Business Profile optimization
Location page content improvements
Review acquisition strategy
Citation consistency cleanup

## Execution
Analysts standardized business categories across all listings.
Service descriptions were rewritten to include primary legal specialties.
Images were added to listings to improve engagement.
Location pages were expanded with city‑specific legal content.
Structured data markup was added for local business entities.

## Outcome
Within three months rankings improved across several high‑value keywords.
Google Business Profile insights showed a 45 percent increase in calls.
Direction requests also increased significantly.
Organic traffic to location pages recovered to previous levels.

## Lessons Learned
Local SEO performance depends heavily on listing completeness and review activity.
Professional service firms often neglect profile maintenance.
Regular audits of local listings can prevent slow visibility decline.

## Transferable Pattern
The same pattern frequently appears in local service industries.
Profiles created during initial marketing efforts often become outdated.
Competitors who maintain active listings gradually gain visibility advantage.
