# Google Business Profile Optimization

## Overview
This service improves the completeness and effectiveness of a
Google Business Profile listing.

## Best Fit
Best for local businesses seeking more calls, visits, or directions
from Google Maps and local search results.

## Deliverables
- optimized profile descriptions
- category updates
- photo additions
- service listings
- review strategy recommendations

## Inputs Required
Access to the GBP listing and business information.

## Typical Findings
Profiles often lack complete service descriptions,
categories, or recent images.

## Exclusions
Does not include review generation campaigns unless requested.

## Prerequisites
The business must have a verified Google Business Profile.

## Success Criteria
Increased profile engagement and local visibility.

## Recommended Pairings
Often combined with local SEO foundation work.

## Notes
Active profile management strengthens local search prominence.
