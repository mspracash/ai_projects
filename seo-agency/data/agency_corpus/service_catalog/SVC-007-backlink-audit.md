# Backlink Audit

## Overview
A backlink audit evaluates the quality and safety of inbound links.

Poor‑quality backlinks may harm rankings or create manual penalty risks.

## Best Fit
Recommended when rankings fluctuate unexpectedly or when a site
has previously engaged in aggressive link‑building campaigns.

## Deliverables
- backlink quality classification
- toxic domain identification
- disavow recommendations
- link profile summary

## Inputs Required
Access to backlink analysis tools or link export data.

## Typical Findings
Large volumes of low‑quality directories,
spam networks, or irrelevant domains.

## Exclusions
Does not include link acquisition campaigns.

## Prerequisites
Backlink data from analysis tools.

## Success Criteria
Reduced risk of penalties and improved link profile health.

## Recommended Pairings
Often paired with technical SEO audits.

## Notes
Regular link monitoring helps maintain a healthy backlink profile.
