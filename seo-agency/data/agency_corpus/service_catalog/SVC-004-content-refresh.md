# Content Refresh

## Overview
Content refresh improves existing content that has lost search visibility.

Over time information becomes outdated and competitors publish newer
resources. Refreshing content restores relevance.

## Best Fit
Best used when pages previously ranked well but traffic gradually declined.

## Deliverables
- updated statistics and references
- expanded topical coverage
- improved headings
- refreshed metadata
- additional internal links

## Inputs Required
Historical traffic data
Search Console impressions
Keyword ranking history

## Typical Findings
Outdated references, shallow content, or competitor pages
with stronger coverage.

## Exclusions
Does not replace full content strategy development.

## Prerequisites
Pages must already exist and have historical performance data.

## Success Criteria
Improved impressions and restored rankings.

## Recommended Pairings
Often paired with keyword research and on‑page optimization.

## Notes
Regular updates help maintain long‑term competitiveness.
