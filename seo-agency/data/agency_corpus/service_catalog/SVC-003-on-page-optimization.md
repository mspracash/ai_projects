# On‑Page Optimization

## Overview
On‑page optimization improves the structure and content of individual
pages so that search engines and users can understand them more clearly.

## Best Fit
Recommended when pages rank but fail to achieve strong visibility
or click‑through rates.

## Deliverables
- optimized page titles
- improved headings
- structured internal linking
- updated metadata
- enhanced page structure

## Inputs Required
Access to page content
Keyword targets
Analytics and search performance data

## Typical Findings
Pages often contain weak heading structure,
missing internal links, or unclear keyword targeting.

## Exclusions
Does not include full content rewriting unless separately scoped.

## Prerequisites
Keyword research should be completed beforehand.

## Success Criteria
Higher ranking stability and improved click‑through rates.

## Recommended Pairings
Works well with keyword research and content refresh.

## Notes
On‑page optimization ensures each page clearly communicates
its relevance to search engines.
