# Local SEO Foundation

## Overview
Local SEO foundation work ensures a business appears consistently
across local search platforms and directories.

## Best Fit
Recommended for businesses targeting geographic search results.

## Deliverables
- citation audit
- NAP consistency checks
- local landing page review
- directory profile updates

## Inputs Required
Business location information
Existing listings and profiles

## Typical Findings
Inconsistent contact information and incomplete directory listings.

## Exclusions
Does not include paid advertising management.

## Prerequisites
Business must have clear location information.

## Success Criteria
Improved local search visibility and listing consistency.

## Recommended Pairings
Often paired with Google Business Profile optimization.

## Notes
Local signals are essential for map‑based search visibility.
