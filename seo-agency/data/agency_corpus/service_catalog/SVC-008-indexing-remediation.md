# Indexing Remediation

## Overview
Indexing remediation resolves issues preventing search engines
from properly indexing a website.

## Best Fit
Used when important pages fail to appear in search results.

## Deliverables
- indexing diagnostics
- robots directive corrections
- sitemap updates
- canonical improvements

## Inputs Required
Search Console access
Site crawl results

## Typical Findings
Noindex tags, blocked resources, or misconfigured canonical tags.

## Exclusions
Does not include large‑scale site redesigns.

## Prerequisites
A crawlable website environment.

## Success Criteria
Restored indexing and improved crawl efficiency.

## Recommended Pairings
Commonly paired with technical SEO audits.

## Notes
Proper indexing is essential for organic visibility.
