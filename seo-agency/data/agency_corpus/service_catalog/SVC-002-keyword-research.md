# Keyword Research

## Overview
Keyword research identifies the search queries that users enter when
looking for products, services, or information.

Understanding these queries helps organizations align their content
with real search demand.

## Best Fit
This service is used when a website lacks clear keyword targeting,
or when rankings decline due to misalignment with user intent.

## Deliverables
Deliverables typically include:
- keyword opportunity lists
- topic clusters
- search intent classification
- competitor keyword analysis

## Inputs Required
Access to analytics data
Existing keyword rankings
Competitor websites for comparison

## Typical Findings
Research often reveals gaps between the topics a website covers
and the topics users search for.

## Exclusions
Keyword research does not automatically include content creation.

## Prerequisites
A clear understanding of the client's products, services, or audience.

## Success Criteria
Improved keyword targeting and better alignment with search intent.

## Recommended Pairings
Often paired with content refresh or on‑page optimization.

## Notes
Effective keyword research provides the strategic foundation
for long‑term organic growth.
