# Monthly SEO Retainer

## Overview
The monthly SEO retainer provides ongoing search optimization services
to maintain and improve a website’s organic performance.

Unlike one-time projects, a retainer engagement allows the agency to
continuously monitor search trends, analyze performance data, and
implement improvements over time.

## Best Fit
This service is appropriate for clients who require continuous
optimization rather than a one-time SEO project.

Typical use cases include:
- sustained traffic growth initiatives
- ongoing ranking improvements
- competitive markets requiring continuous optimization

## Deliverables
Deliverables typically include:
- monthly SEO performance analysis
- keyword ranking monitoring
- ongoing on-page optimization
- technical SEO recommendations
- content strategy adjustments

## Inputs Required
Access to analytics and search console data  
Access to the website content management system  
Historical SEO reports if available

## Typical Findings
Ongoing monitoring often reveals:
- new keyword opportunities
- emerging competitor strategies
- gradual ranking declines that require corrective action
- technical issues affecting search visibility

## Exclusions
The retainer does not automatically include large technical
development projects or complete site redesigns.

Major engineering work may require separate project scopes.

## Prerequisites
Initial diagnostic work such as a technical audit or keyword research
is recommended before starting a long-term retainer engagement.

This ensures the agency begins with a clear strategic direction.

## Success Criteria
Success is measured through:
- sustained growth in organic traffic
- improved keyword rankings
- increased lead or conversion volume from search traffic

## Recommended Pairings
Often paired with:
technical SEO audits  
content optimization services  
keyword research updates

## Notes
Search engine algorithms and competitive environments change
frequently.

A retainer engagement allows the agency to respond to these
changes proactively rather than reacting after performance
declines occur.
