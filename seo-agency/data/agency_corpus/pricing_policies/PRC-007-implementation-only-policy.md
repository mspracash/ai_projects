# Implementation‑Only Policy

## Purpose
Defines when the agency may perform implementation without diagnostic analysis.

## Client Requests
Clients sometimes request only technical changes such as fixing errors or updating metadata.

## Risk Considerations
Without investigation, implementation may target the wrong problem.

## Acceptable Situations
Implementation‑only engagements may proceed when scope is clearly defined and low risk.

## Documentation
Client acknowledgement should be recorded if diagnostic work is declined.
