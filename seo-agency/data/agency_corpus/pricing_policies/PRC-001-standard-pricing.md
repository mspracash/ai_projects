# Standard Pricing Policy

## Purpose
This document defines the baseline pricing structure used for SEO engagements.
Standard pricing ensures proposals accurately reflect the effort required to diagnose and resolve search performance issues.

## Pricing Philosophy
SEO work consists of two main phases:
1. Diagnostic analysis
2. Implementation

Separating these stages helps clients understand the value of investigation before solutions are applied.

## Diagnostic Services
Diagnostic work may include:
- Technical SEO audit
- Keyword research
- Competitive analysis
- Site architecture review

These activities identify the root cause of ranking or traffic problems.

## Implementation Services
Once issues are diagnosed, implementation may involve:
- On‑page optimization
- Technical fixes
- Internal linking improvements
- Content restructuring

Pricing depends on the scope of implementation work.

## Proposal Structure
Standard proposals should include:
- Diagnostic phase
- Recommended implementation
- Estimated timeline

This structure reflects the logical order of SEO work.

## Transparency
Clients should understand:
- what each service does
- why it is necessary
- how it affects outcomes

Transparent pricing increases trust and proposal acceptance.

## Adjustments
Pricing may change when:
- the site is unusually large
- technical complexity is high
- the engagement requires custom research

Adjustments must remain consistent with agency guidelines.

## Documentation
All pricing decisions must be documented, including:
- services included
- pricing adjustments
- approval notes
