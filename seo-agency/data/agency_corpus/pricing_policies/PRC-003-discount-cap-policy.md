# Discount Cap Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines how discounts may be applied without compromising diagnostic work.

## Core Rule
Discounts must not remove critical investigative services required to understand a client’s problem.

## Applicability
Applies to engagements involving traffic decline or local visibility issues.

## Common Scenarios
Discount requests often occur when clients compare agencies or ask for pricing adjustments.

## Boundary Conditions
Budget limitations may affect implementation scope, but diagnostic services should remain.

## Exceptions
Exceptions may occur if the client provides a credible recent audit.

## Approval Thresholds
Large discounts require review by a senior strategist or pricing lead.

## Diagnostic Expectations
Investigating root causes is mandatory before recommending remediation.

## Bundle Interaction
Discounts applied to bundles must maintain logical service sequencing.

## Example
If a client asks to remove an audit to lower cost, analysts should explain why the audit is necessary.
