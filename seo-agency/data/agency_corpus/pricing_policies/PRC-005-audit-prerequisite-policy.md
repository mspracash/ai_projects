# Audit Prerequisite Policy

## Purpose
Defines when a technical audit must occur before implementation.

## Why Audits Matter
Search visibility problems can originate from many technical factors.

## Mandatory Cases
Audits are required when:
- traffic declines suddenly
- rankings drop across many pages
- major site migrations occur

## Audit Scope
Typical audit tasks include crawl analysis, indexation review, and architecture inspection.

## Implementation Relationship
Implementation should follow the audit results.

## External Audits
Client‑provided audits may be reviewed and validated before use.

## Documentation
Audit findings must be documented clearly.
