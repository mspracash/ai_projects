# Diagnostic‑First Engagement Policy

## Purpose
Establishes that diagnostic analysis should occur before major SEO implementation.

## Rationale
Traffic loss can result from multiple causes including technical issues, algorithm changes, or content relevance.

## Diagnostic Activities
Typical investigations include:
- technical audits
- backlink analysis
- content gap analysis

## Engagement Flow
1 Investigation
2 Analysis
3 Recommendation
4 Implementation

## Benefits
Diagnostic‑first engagements reduce misdirected work and improve long‑term performance.

## Client Education
Clients should understand that investigation prevents wasted implementation effort.
