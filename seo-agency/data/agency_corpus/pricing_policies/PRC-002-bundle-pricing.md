# Bundle Pricing Policy

## Purpose
Bundle pricing allows multiple SEO services to be combined into a single engagement package.

## Concept
Bundles group related services that support a common objective.

Example bundle:
- Keyword research
- Content optimization
- Internal linking

## Benefits
Bundles simplify proposals and allow coordinated implementation across services.

## Design Principles
Bundles should focus on a specific goal such as:
- content improvement
- technical remediation
- local SEO visibility

## Pricing Structure
Bundle pricing should be lower than the total cost of individual services but still maintain agency margins.

## Diagnostic Integration
Bundles should include diagnostic work when the root cause of an issue is unknown.

## Client Communication
Clients should understand how services inside the bundle work together to improve results.

## Monitoring
Bundle performance should be monitored and adjusted when necessary.
