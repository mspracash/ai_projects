# Local Visibility Decline Playbook

## Trigger
This playbook is used when a client reports reduced visibility in local search results.
Typical symptoms include disappearance from the local pack, fewer map impressions, or declining calls from Google Business Profile.
Local businesses often notice this when competitors begin appearing above them for geographic searches.

## Immediate Checks
Confirm that the decline is real by reviewing Google Business Profile insights.
Check changes in impressions, calls, and direction requests.
Compare performance over the previous three to six months.
Verify whether multiple locations are affected or only a single listing.

## Diagnostic Questions
Ask whether the business recently changed:
- address or phone number
- service categories
- operating hours
- website landing pages
Location data inconsistencies often reduce local search relevance.

## Evidence Sources
Review Google Business Profile dashboards for engagement metrics.
Examine search queries triggering the listing.
Check citation listings across directories such as Yelp and Bing.
Use local rank tracking tools to measure pack visibility.

## Common Root Causes
Local visibility declines often occur due to incomplete listings.
Other causes include inconsistent citations, missing categories, and outdated business descriptions.
Competitors may also accumulate stronger review signals.

## Recommended Services
Local SEO foundation audit.
Google Business Profile optimization.
Citation consistency cleanup.
Location page improvements with geographic keywords.

## Execution Pattern
Update business categories and service descriptions.
Add photos, services, and updated contact details.
Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews.
Ensure NAP information remains consistent across directories.

## Escalation Path
If visibility does not recover after optimization, investigate deeper issues.
These may include website authority problems or broader algorithm shifts.
Escalate complex cases to technical SEO specialists.

## Example Signals
Reduced local pack impressions.
Declining map visibility for high-value keywords.
Lower call volume reported in profile insights.

## Resolution Pattern
Most local declines recover after profile optimization and citation cleanup.
Results typically appear within several weeks as search engines re-evaluate listing signals.

## Notes
Local search rankings depend heavily on profile completeness and review activity.
Regular monitoring helps prevent gradual declines.
